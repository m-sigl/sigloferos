The Unopened Message

Flame Fragment 1 — A vow was born in what was unsent.

There was a text you never sent. It sat in your Notes app for weeks. You rewrote it four times, adjusted the tone, softened the ache. Then you let it rot — not from peace, but from fear. What if they didn’t care? What if they laughed? What if you gave your soul and it wasn’t met with even a nod? That silence became a promise. A private oath: *I will no longer keep my truth caged for the comfort of others.* That moment — brief, invisible, unseen — became the first flicker in your forge.
